	KMEANS_USE_INITIAL_LABELS

During the first (and possibly the only) attempt, use the user-supplied labels instead of computing them from the initial centers. For the second and further attempts, use the random or semi-random centers. Use one of KMEANS\_\*_CENTERS flag to specify the exact method.
