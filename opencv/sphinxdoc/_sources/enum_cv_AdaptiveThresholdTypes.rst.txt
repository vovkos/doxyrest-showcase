.. ref-code-block:: cpp
	:class: doxyrest-title-code-block

	ADAPTIVE_THRESH_GAUSSIAN_C

the threshold value :math:`T(x, y)` is a weighted sum (cross-correlation with a Gaussian window) of the :math:`\texttt{blockSize} \times \texttt{blockSize}` neighborhood of :math:`(x, y)` minus C . The default sigma (standard deviation) is used for the specified blockSize . See :ref:`cv::getGaussianKernel <doxid-d4/d86/group__imgproc__filter_1gac05a120c1ae92a6060dd0db190a61afa>`
