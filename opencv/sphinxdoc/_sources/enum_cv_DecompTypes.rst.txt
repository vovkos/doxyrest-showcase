	DECOMP_NORMAL

while all the previous flags are mutually exclusive, this flag can be used together with any of the previous; it means that the normal equations :math:`\texttt{src1}^T\cdot\texttt{src1}\cdot\texttt{dst}=\texttt{src1}^T\texttt{src2}` are solved instead of the original system :math:`\texttt{src1}\cdot\texttt{dst}=\texttt{src2}`
