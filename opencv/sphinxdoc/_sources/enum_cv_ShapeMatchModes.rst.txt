Detailed Documentation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Shape matching methods.

:math:`A` denotes object1, :math:`B` denotes object2

:math:`\begin{array}{l} m^A_i = \mathrm{sign} (h^A_i) \cdot \log{h^A_i} \\ m^B_i = \mathrm{sign} (h^B_i) \cdot \log{h^B_i} \end{array}`

and :math:`h^A_i, h^B_i` are the Hu moments of :math:`A` and :math:`B`, respectively.
