Detailed Documentation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

This class is used for reading E-Mail messages from a POP3 server.

All occurrences of "\r\n..\r\n" are replaced with "\r\n.\r\n". The first occurrence of "\r\n.\r\n" denotes the end of the stream.
