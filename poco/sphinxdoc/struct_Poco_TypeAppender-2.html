TypeAppender appends T (type or a TypeList) to Head.

Usage:

typedef TypeListType<char>::HeadType Type1; typedef TypeAppender<Type1, int>::HeadType Type2; (Type2 is a TypeList of char,int)

typedef TypeListType<float, double>::HeadType Type3; typedef TypeAppender<Type2, Type3>::HeadType Type4; (Type4 is a TypeList of char,int,float,double)
